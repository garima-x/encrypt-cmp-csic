Sensitive Personal Data Notice — DPDP Act 2023, Section 4 & 6
Filing an FIR requires processing of sensitive personal data including your biometric information, precise location data, financial details (in fraud cases), health records (in assault cases), and identity documents. Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the right to provide explicit, granular consent for each category of data and withdraw it at any time. Your data will not be shared with third parties beyond investigating agencies without separate consent.
